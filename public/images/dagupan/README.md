# Dagupan Images

Place your Dagupan images in this directory with the following filenames:

## Required Images
1. `welcome-sign.jpg` - Welcome to Dagupan sign with fish
2. `i-love-dagupan.jpg` - I ❤️ Dagupan sign
3. `beach-scene.jpg` - Beach with people
4. `beach-aerial.jpg` - Aerial view of Dagupan beach
5. `city-map.jpg` - Dagupan City map
6. `festival-dancers.jpg` - Bangus festival dancers
7. `festival-performers.jpg` - Festival performers with fish
8. `festival-parade.jpg` - Festival parade
9. `market-square.jpg` - CSI Market Square
10. `sm-center.jpg` - SM Center Dagupan
11. `street-scene.jpg` - Dagupan street view
12. `jeepneys.jpg` - Colorful jeepneys
13. `train.jpg` - Historic train
14. `church-aerial.jpg` - Church aerial view
15. `church-building.jpg` - Church facade

## Usage
Images are referenced in the views as `/images/dagupan/[filename]`

## Note
If an image is missing, the design will gracefully fall back to CSS-generated graphics.
